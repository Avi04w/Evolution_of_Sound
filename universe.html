Universe of Hits

Explore 15,000+ songs visualized in 3D space using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) of audio features from Spotify.

Each point represents a song. PCA reduces seven audio features (energy, danceability, valence, etc.) into three dimensions for visualization. The specific axes don't have direct meaning—instead, PCA finds patterns that group similar songs together. Songs close to each other share similar acoustic characteristics, revealing hidden patterns in music.

Interactions:

  • Click and drag to rotate
  • Cmd/Ctrl + Scroll to zoom
  • Hover over points for details
  • Click a point to play the song

Filters & Controls

Use the persistent feature selector at the top left of the page to color the points by different audio features.

Understanding the Space: PCA Loading Vectors

You may have noticed that certain audio features seem to increase in specific directions. These are the PCA loading vectors—arrows showing how each musical characteristic maps onto our 3D space.

For example, if you color by "Energy" and enable the loading vectors below, you'll see the energy arrow pointing in the direction where songs become more energetic.

Feel free to scroll back up and verify!
